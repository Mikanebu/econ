Where Does My Money Go? - Home

Ever wondered as a UK citizen where your money goes when you pay taxes –
what exactly it gets spent on, who exactly receives it?

We're here to help you find out

We need your help!

Interested in where your money goes? Want to help us extend and improve this project? Then get in touch by email (or otherwise). We need help with all kinds of things such as:

  • Suggestions as to what data we should be looking at (and where we can find it)
  • Help with processing data and building compelling visualizations
  • Taking our findings to citizens and the media

To get started take a look at:

  • Spending by Department
  • Spending by Function
  • Total Government Spending

A Note on Sources

This data comes from Public Expenditure Statistics Publications published by HM Treasury. Our "packaged" version of the data, which includes all the code to get the data from human readable spreadsheets to something more useful to a computer, can be found at http://knowledgeforge.net/econ/hg/file/tip/econdata/ukgovfinances. We've also uploaded all of PESA (plus summary) to a Google docs spreadsheet for easy viewing and editing.
